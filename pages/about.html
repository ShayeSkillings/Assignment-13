About Eddystone Rock & Ready Mix

Eddystone Rock & Ready Mix is a leading supplier of high-quality concrete products in the Ketchikan, Alaska. With years of experience and a commitment to quality, we provide reliable solutions for we provide reliable solutions for both residential and commercial projects.

Our company takes pride in our ability to deliver top-notch products and exceptional service. Our team of skilled professionals works tirelessly to ensure that every batch of concrete meets the highest standards of quality and consistency.

Whether you're a homeowner, contractor, or developer, you can trust Eddystone Rock and Ready Mix for all your concrete needs. From foundations to driveways, sidewalks to retaining walls, we have the expertise and resources to handle projects of any size and complexity.
